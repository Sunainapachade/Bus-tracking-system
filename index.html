What is bus tracking system?

The bus tracking system uses GPS technology and software applications to track the location of buses and provide real-time updates to operators and passengers. These systems allow passengers to track buses in real-time, making sure they know exactly when the next bus will arrive.

What are the benefits of bus tracking system?

The absence of information can have a poor impact on the business bottom line. It also prevents management from identifying opportunities and systematic problems for improvement. A real time bus tracker can help resolve those problems by offering insights and critical information to fleet managers and owners.
